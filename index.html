How is this calculated?

This metric represents the total revenue generated from all payment transactions during the selected time period.

Metrics calculations

Total committed revenue

A sum of your billing revenue and usage charges measured by meters. Usage and billing revenue have been committed by customers but may not have yet been collected. Decide whether discounts, credits, and uncollected committed revenue are included in your calculation.

Monthly recurring revenue

Include revenue that is committed in total revenue but has not yet been collected

One time discounts

Include one time discounts in your total revenue

Free credits

Include free credits in your total revenue
